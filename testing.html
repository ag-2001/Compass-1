SAT vs. ACT

What is the SAT and ACT and why are these tests important?
These tests are standardized exams, which are widely used by colleges for college admissions. Although these tests may be intimidating, it is important to take them, since most colleges require them. Which of these tests is right for you? These tests are very similar, but also have some significant differences. If a factor such as time pressure is what you find most difficult, then you should probably take the SAT, because the ACT is more fast-paced. However, if you prefer questions that may be a little longer but are more straightforward and easy to understand, you might want to take the ACT. The biggest difference between SAT and ACT is that the ACT has a Science Reasoning section. You can determine which test is best for you by taking both an SAT and ACT practice test and see which one you find easier. Here is a chart with each of the sections and its time, for both of the tests.

What are some strategies for taking these tests?

The most important thing to remember when you take these is test is that there is no penalty for guessing. Therefore, if you are running out of time or just do not know what the answer is, you should guess because you have a 25% chance of getting it right. Also, take several full-length practice tests before you take the exam. Take your practice tests in a test environment- in a place with no distractions. Eat a proper breakfast before your test and bring snacks and water for the breaks in between the sections of the test. This will ensure that you remain focused during the exam.

How do I study for the SAT/ACT?
It is crucial to take a lot of practice tests when studying for the SAT/ACT. Practice tests ensure that you understand the format of the test and that you know the types of problems that will be given on the test. You do not want to end up getting surprised on test day. College Board has 8 released SAT tests online. You may either use the online versions for free, or can buy The Official SAT Guide, 2018 Edition. For the ACT, ACT has released 5 practice tests, for which you can find the practice tests online for free or by the official book: ACT Prep Book 2017-2018: Test Prep Book & Practice Test Questions for the English, Math, Reading, Science, and Writing Sections of the ACT Exam.

1. One of the best free websites to use to study for SAT is Khan Academy. Khan Academy offers personalized practice based on your needs. Although this may not be enough to guarantee your goal score, it is free and does help strengthen the skills you are struggling with.

AP Exams
AP courses are a great way to challenge yourself in your academia. Remember, AP courses in high school are equivalent to courses in college in terms of difficulty, so taking an excessive amount of AP courses can actually be counterproductive to you. If there is a particular AP course that your school offers and that interests you, it may be worth the extra work to take the course. Talk to the teachers at your school who teach the course and ask them for more information.

AP tests are an opportunity for you to skip certain classes in college. After taking an AP course or self studying for AP tests, you can opt to take an AP test in order to skip that introductory course in college and move on to more challenging courses. An AP test is scored out of 5, and it involves a series of multiple choice and free response questions, depending on the subject. Generally a 3,4, and 5 are accepted by schools in order to skip the course in college, however be sure to review the college’s website to see what they accept.
