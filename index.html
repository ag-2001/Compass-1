Our Mission

Welcome to Compass, a site for high schoolers by high schoolers solely for the purpose of providing a myriad of resources that will guide students through high school on to a college bound path. Throughout this website you will find information about financial aid, summer programs, SAT and ACT testing, A-G requirements, study skills, and resume writing. We hope this site will serve as a useful reference during your time in high school.
